## Key Decision Points Covered in Docs

### Why Agentic AI?
**Docs:** README.md + AGENTIC_AI_GUIDE.md
**Answer:** Because software is moving from tools → agents. Learning agentic patterns now makes you valuable.

### Why Ask My Notes?
**Docs:** askmynotes/README.md + AGENTIC_AI_GUIDE.md
**Answer:** Foundation for agentic thinking + portfolio project + interview talking points all in one.

### Why Gemini → Ollama?
**Docs:** INTERVIEW_QUESTIONS.md Q2 + README.md
**Answer:** Hit API limits, pivoted to local inference for better portfolio story + unlimited requests.

### Why 384-dim embeddings?
**Docs:** INTERVIEW_QUESTIONS.md Q4 + ARCHITECTURE.md
**Answer:** Trade-off between quality, speed, and memory. Perfect for M4 MacBook.

### Why temperature 0.3?
**Docs:** INTERVIEW_QUESTIONS.md Q5 + ARCHITECTURE.md
**Answer:** Balance between deterministic (factual) and creative. Prevents hallucinations.

### Why cosine similarity?
**Docs:** INTERVIEW_QUESTIONS.md Q3 + ARCHITECTURE.md
**Answer:** Cares about semantic direction, not magnitude. Perfect for comparing meaning.
